About

My story as an artist

I'm a music artist and producer from Ontario, Canada. Music isn't just what I do—it's who I am.

My journey started with a deep love for rhythm and melody. Over the years, I've poured my soul into creating music that resonates with people on a personal level. Every track I produce carries a piece of my story and emotions.

When I'm not in the studio creating, you'll find me connecting with other artists, performing live, or finding inspiration in everyday moments. My goal is to create music that matters—music that moves you.
